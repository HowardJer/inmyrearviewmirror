WHY HAVE I CREATED THIS BLOG?

Vanity?
Self-Serving?

I don't know.
But in a world where reaching 100 years is no longer uncommon,
I find myself well into the final trimester of my life.
I find myself reminiscing of all the years gone by.
And I see all the greats of history having left their indelible footprints in the sands of time.
And I look back and find that all my footprints have been washed away by lapping waves.

I have not achieved greatness but I feel that whoever I met or befriended has been affected by knowing me.
Jus as I am the sum of all those I have met or became friends with. 

so no, I will not show up in future history books and records of my life will disappear once anyone that has known has passed as well.

So I created this blog, more of a memoir actually, for 2 reasons.

just to put a written voice to my memories that fill more and more of my days.
And to learn.
I never want to stop learning.
With the introductions of AI I can now figure out how to create a website.
I took a course  but like all the other in my class it was so compressed it was hard to learn and retain the lessons.
With AI I am constructing this website to reinforce the lessons I learned.

And in the end maybe have my son read this blog and learn a little of what his father was.
And maybe if someone reads these words they too will remember things that have passes away into the analog world of the forgotten.
